# Achievements

A Navidrome plugin that unlocks achievements based on your listening history.

## Features

- Listening milestones
- Artist, album, and track achievements
- Listening streaks
- Time-of-day achievements
- Progress stored per user

## Installation

Requires Navidrome with plugin support enabled.

1. Download `achievements.ndp` from the latest release.
2. Copy it into your Navidrome plugins directory.
3. Restart Navidrome if necessary.
4. Enable the plugin from **Settings → Plugins** and grant it access to the desired users.


## Achievements

Current achievements include:

- Needle Drop: Scrobble your very first track.
- Finding the Groove: Reach 10 scrobbles.
- Triple Digits: Reach 100 scrobbles.
- Halfway to Obsession: Reach 500 scrobbles.
- Thousand Track Stare: Reach 1,000 scrobbles.
- Endless Rotation: Reach 5,000 scrobbles.
- Midnight Mixtape: Scrobble 20 tracks between midnight and 4am.
- Sunrise Session: Scrobble 20 tracks between 5am and 7am.
- Weekend Rotation: Scrobble 50 tracks on weekends.
- Devoted Fan: Play the same artist 50 times.
- Stuck on Repeat: Play the same track 20 times.
- Crate Digger: Scrobble tracks from 100 different artists.
- Deep Cuts: Scrobble tracks from 50 different albums.
- All-Day Listener: Accumulate over 24 hours of total listening time.
- Seven-Day Spin: Listen to music 7 days in a row.
- Habit Formed: Listen to music 30 days in a row.
- Extended Play: Scrobble a track 10 minutes or longer.

More achievements are planned.
